Kowel | Kovel | Ковель | קאָוועל‎ | Ко́вель

Research, Exploration, Documentation of a city that was largely destroyed in WW1 and again in WW2

Kowel is a city that goes by many names due to it's rich history of being populated with various ethnicities and nationalities.

What's the point of this project?

  1. attempt to reconstruct the city as a 3D model as it appeared prior to WW2 & WW1
  2. collect images related to the city as it appeared and as it apppears today
  3. create a database that will allow anyone to search for historical information on the city

Who is involved in this project?

This project is a cooperative effort between Martin Kraszewski and the team behind Kowel.vintage (an instagram page dedicated to the history of the city).
