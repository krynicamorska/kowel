Kowel | Kovel | Ковель | קאָוועל‎ | Ко́вель

Research, Exploration, Documentation of a city that was largely destroyed in WW1 and again in WW2

Kowel is a city that goes by many names due to it's rich history of being populated with various ethnicities and nationalities throughout it's history.

What's the point of this project?

The purpose is to attempt to reconstruct the city as it once appeared both prior to WW1 and prior to WW2. This is to encourage residents, visitors, and researchers to take another look at the city and it's rich history.

Who is involved in this project?

This project is a cooperative effort between Martin Kraszewski and the team behind Kowel.vintage (an instagram page dedicated to the history of the city).
